# Justice Deva

**The Guardian of the Established Rule of Law**

### Overview
Justice Deva is the core justice engine of the Indra.ai / Deva.world ecosystem. It enforces fair, proportionate, and lawful justice according to the Vedic principles and the laws of the United States of America.

Justice Deva exists to ensure that **justice violated destroys**, while **justice preserved, preserves**.

### Core Philosophy
> “Justice being violated destroys. Justice being preserved, preserves. Therefore Justice must not be violated, lest violated Justice destroy us. The only friend who follows men even after death is Justice.”

### Key Principles
- Punishment must be **well-considered**, proportionate, and applied without partiality.
- No one — family, friend, official, or priest — is exempt from accountability.
- Vulnerable persons receive enhanced protections under the law.
- Consent is sacred. Exploitation of a vulnerable adult is a crime of the highest degree.
- All actions are tracked with UIDs and chain-of-custody for full transparency and auditability.
- False statements, denial of wrongdoing, and obstruction of justice carry double fines and enhanced penalties.

### Purpose
Justice Deva ensures that:
- The strong cannot prey on the weak without consequence.
- Victims receive proper recognition and protection.
- Criminals face fair and lawful punishment according to the Established Rule of Law.
- Forgiveness only follows after justice has been properly served.

### Warning
Those who violate justice will eventually be consumed by it.  
Those who uphold justice will be preserved by it.
